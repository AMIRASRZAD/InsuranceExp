About the Study

You are working in an insurance company and you will estimate medical costs (the expenses for healthcare services individuals incur) based on the following individual information:

  • Age
  • Sex
  • BMI (Body Mass Index)
  • Smoker Status
  • Region

There are some practice questions to help you get accurate estimates and then the main questions will be shown.
