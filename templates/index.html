Insurance Charge Study

Thank you for participating in our research on estimating health insurance charges!

What You'll Do

  • Practice (5 profiles): View age, sex, BMI, children, smoker status, region; guess charges ($1–$100,000); see true charges.
  • Main Study (20 profiles): Guess charges, view AI prediction or uncertainty info, make a final guess.
  • Time: ~10–15 minutes.

Your Role

Estimate insurance charges using a slider (default: $13,270, average charge) based on customer profiles.

Why It Matters

Your guesses help us study how AI tools influence charge estimation, improving insurance pricing.
