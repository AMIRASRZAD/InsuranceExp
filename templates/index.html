Medical Cost Estimation Study

Thank you for participating in our research to estimate medical costs!

About the Study

You will estimate medical costs—the expenses for healthcare services individuals incur. You’ll base your estimates on the following individual information:

  • Age
  • Sex
  • BMI (Body Mass Index)
  • Number of Children
  • Smoker Status
  • Region of Residence (Northeast, Northwest, Southeast, Southwest)

Practice (10 Individuals)

To help you make accurate estimates, you’ll review information for 10 individuals and see their actual medical charges.

Main Study

You’ll view the same type of information as in practice, estimate charges using a slider (default: $13,270, average charge), then see AI predictions and information (not necessarily true charges). You can adjust your estimate afterward. Your performance will be evaluated based on the accuracy of your estimates.

Time

~10–15 minutes
